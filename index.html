Spacewarweb

This game is an homage to the classic game Spacewar created by the first hackers of the MIT AI Lab in 1962 using a PDP-1 computer.

Instructions

You can combine your bullets to create a rift that damage the opponent over time.
Watch out for the gravitational pull of the sun. Or use it to your advantage.

Controls:

WASD -> Control Player 1
Arrow Keys -> Control Player 2
E | . -> Create a Rift
P | ESC -> Pause / Unpause
R -> Restart
